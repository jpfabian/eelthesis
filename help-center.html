Frequently Asked Questions

What is EEL?

EEL (English Enhancement Learning) is an AI-powered platform designed to help students improve their English literacy through reading, pronunciation, and comprehension exercises.

How do I join a class?

Once you've created a student account, your teacher will provide you with a unique Class ID. Enter this ID in your dashboard to join the class.

Is EEL free to use?

EEL is currently a thesis project developed for educational purposes at Norzagaray National High School and is free for its students and teachers.

Can I use EEL on my phone?

Yes! EEL is designed to be fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and computers.
