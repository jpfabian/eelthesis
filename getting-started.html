Getting Started

Follow these steps to quickly set up your EEL account and start your learning journey.

For Teachers

Step 1: Sign Up - Create your teacher account by clicking the "Sign Up" button and selecting "Teacher" role.

Step 2: Create a Class - Once logged in, click "Create Class" to generate a unique Class ID for your students.

Step 3: Add Lessons - Explore the lesson library or use the AI generator to create customized English literacy quizzes.

Step 4: Monitor Progress - Share the Class ID with your students and track their performance in real-time.

Create Teacher Account

For Students

Step 1: Create Account - Register as a "Student" using your email and a strong password.

Step 2: Join a Class - Enter the Class ID provided by your teacher to access your assigned materials.

Step 3: Start Quizzes - Begin with reading comprehension or pronunciation lessons assigned to your class.

Step 4: Track Progress - View your scores and see your improvements over time in the student dashboard.
